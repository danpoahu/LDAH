Aloha! LDAH (Leadership in Disabilities and Achievement of Hawaiʻi) is working to better understand how Native Hawaiian families experience and access special education information. Your responses will help us identify gaps in translated materials, family-friendly resources, and culturally relevant supports for children with disabilities and their ʻohana.

This survey is anonymous — no names or contact information are collected. All answers are optional.
